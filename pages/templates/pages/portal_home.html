Your Profile

E-Learning & CBT Access

Result Upload
Teachers can upload academic results for their assigned subjects and classes, including Continuous Assessment (CA) and examination scores. Subject teachers can only enter results for the subjects they teach, while form teachers have broader access and may upload results for all students in their class.
Attendance Management
Teachers can take and update daily attendance for their classes using the attendance tools available in the portal.
Student Information Access
The portal allows teachers to view relevant student details for their assigned classes, enabling better communication and academic support.
Upload E-Learning Materials
